How to Create a Meaningful Churn Analysis

Learn customer churn approaches to better understand how to use and interpret current data.

Steps to Create a Meaningful Churn Analysis

Define Churn: Clearly define how a company determines churn. This may include a customer not making a purchase for a specific period or when they cancel a subscrption.

Benchmark Churn Thresholds: Determine what is an "acceptable" amount of churn based on the company. For example, retail can have a higher turnover while other specialzed industries (such as healthcare) can have devastatng consequences with even a 1% churn rate.

Collect the Right Data: Collect customer data which may include, but is not limited to: customer support interactions, transaction hstroy, demographics, and other relevant information.

Prepare the Data: Determine how to handle missing values, inconsistencies in the data, outliers, and more.

Perform Exploratory Analysis: Explore the data statistically and/or visually. This helps us understand customer behavior patterns, demographs, and interactions with services or products.

Choose the Right Features: Indentify significant variables that could influence churn. Some examples may be customer support interactions, purchasing frequency, and more.

Segement Customers: Categorize or group customers based on geography, demographcs, behavior, etc.

Choose a Predictive Modeling Technique: Use machine learning algorithms to predict churn. Train the model using the historical data and test it;s accuracy with validation datasets.

Identify Churn Indicators: Identify which various strongly correlate with customer churn.

Analyze Feedback Separately: From customer survey data, determine common ssues customers face to better address the root causes behind churn.

Suggest Retention Stategies: Based on the analysis, suggest targeted retention strategies such as improving customer services, product enhancements, loyalty programs, personalized marketing campaigns, etc.

Moderate and Iterate: Continuously monitor the impact of retention strategies. Analyze and refine strategies based on current customer feedback and new data insights.
